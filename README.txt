How-to install:
Place the widget and the Google spreadsheet class in the Wordpress/wp-conten/plugins folder. Then surf in to your Wordpress dashboard. Activate the widget as a plug-in.
Then go to the widget control panel and drag it to your widget side panel. Open up the widget options panel and enter the connection info.
It is important that you enter the correct information otherwise wont your Wordpress page show correctly save your settings and surf to you Wordpress front page.

Dependencies:
Google ZendGdata
Google spreadsheet class.
Php
Wordpress

Make sure that the spreadsheet class is in the plugins folder and that the ZendGdata folder path is in the php.ini or include the path in the google spreadsheet class.
